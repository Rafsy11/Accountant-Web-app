Accountant Web-app 💰
A lightweight, privacy-focused daily finance tracker.

Key Features:
  -Privacy Mode: Hide your balance with a single click.

  -Real-time Kurs: Automatic currency conversion for USD, IDR, and more.

  -Interactive Charts: Visualize your spending with Line, Bar, and Candlestick charts.

  -PWA Ready: Install it on your phone like a real app.

  -Client-Side Only: Your financial data stays in your browser. No databases, no leaks.
